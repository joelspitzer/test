This tip sheet outlines the most effective strategies for quitting smoking and staying quit. Each tip includes a link to more detailed articles or videos on WhyQuit.com.

1. Quit Cold Turkey

In the long run, this is the easiest and most effective technique. Do not carry cigarettes with you.

Learn more about Cold Turkey
2. One Day at a Time

Concentrate on not smoking from the time you wake up until you go to sleep. Don't worry about next week.

Learn more about "One Day at a Time"
3. Drink Fruit Juice

Drink plenty of fruit juice (like cranberry) for the first three days. It helps flush nicotine out of your system.

Why I recommend cranberry juice
4. Practice Deep Breathing

When you have a craving, practice deep breathing exercises. It helps the physical feeling pass more quickly.

How to use deep breathing
5. Handle Triggers

Situations like coffee, alcohol, or social events can trigger urges. Maintain your routine, but be prepared.

Dealing with smoking triggers
6. Avoid Weight Gain

If you feel the need to eat, choose vegetables like celery or carrots instead of candies.

Tips for weight management
7. Crisis Management

If a crisis occurs, remember: smoking is no solution. It only complicates the original problem.

Smoking and stress
The Golden Rule:
NEVER TAKE ANOTHER PUFF!
