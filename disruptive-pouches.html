Below is a post that was made on the QuttingZyn support board on Reddit. The response was actually run through ChatGPT to sanitize the language a bit for use with the general public.

Original Post: Day 3 – this experience is incredible

Discovered this reddit a few days ago and it’s been tremendously helpful – so thanks to everyone contributing. This is my story with the pouches, and why I firmly believe they should not be legal.

Been a nicotine addict for 10 years – first smoking cigs, then started using 10mg VELO pouches... 14 months ago I quit smoking and picked up VELOs (roughly 15-20 pouches a day of 10mg) and since then, my health has significantly deteriorated.

8-10 months ago while at work I came very close to fainting all of a sudden. I started feeling heart palpitations, dizziness, difficulty breathing, difficulty sleeping, restlessness… Went to the doctor who checked EKG, bloodwork, all looked superb. Doc came to the conclusion that I was suffering from anxiety or a similar mental illness, as all my physicals were on point.

Then, on a flight home 4 days ago, I started getting crazy palpitations, becoming dizzy. I decided to ditch the pouches. This is now ~60 hours ago, and to be very honest, I cannot believe how incredible this is. My heart palpitations are COMPLETELY gone. I feel I can breathe 2x the volume of air as I could before.
