Why Informing Your Healthcare Providers of Your Nicotine Pouch Use Can Be Crucial

Many people don't think to tell their doctor about Zyn because they aren't "smoking." However, nicotine significantly affects heart rate, blood pressure, and gastrointestinal health.

Transparency ensures you get the right diagnosis and prevents unnecessary treatments for symptoms that are actually nicotine-driven.
