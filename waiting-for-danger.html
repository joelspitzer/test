Waiting for Zyn or other nicotine pouches to be proven dangerous before you are going to quit

A common sentiment among users of newer nicotine products is the desire to wait for definitive long-term studies. However, waiting for "proof" of danger is a risky gamble with your health.

Don't let yourself be the test subject for these products while the clinical data catches up to the real-world usage.
