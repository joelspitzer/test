Waiting for Zyn and other nicotine pouches to be proven dangerous before you are going to quit?

Many people are taking up Zyn and other nicotine pouch products by being told that it is a relatively safe nicotine alternative and has not been proven to carry many risks. This video discusses why no one should take any solace in these claims of relative safety.

A Lesson from History: Cigarettes

Cigarettes were first mass-produced and marketed as a safe product in the late 19th Century. It took decades of widespread use before the true dangers were officially recognized.

  • 1964: The American public was finally told about the link between cigarettes and lung cancer.
  • 1979: The Surgeon General’s report finally causally tied cigarettes to heart and circulatory disease.
  • 1989: Cigarette smoking was officially deemed the most preventable cause of death in the world.

According to the World Health Organization (WHO), smoking is projected to cause approximately 1 billion deaths in the 21st century. We should not wait for similar statistics to emerge for nicotine pouches before taking action.
